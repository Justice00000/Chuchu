Get Involved and Make a Difference

Join us in our mission to improve menstrual health for millions.

At Chuchu Softies, we believe in the power of collective action to create meaningful change. Our mission to improve menstrual health for millions across Africa and beyond is made possible by the dedication and passion of volunteers like you.

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • Impact: By volunteering with Chuchu Softies, you'll directly contribute to our efforts in distributing menstrual kits, empowering girls and women, and breaking menstrual taboos.
  • Community: Join a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who are committed to making a positive impact on society.
  • Personal Growth: Volunteering offers valuable opportunities for personal and professional development, from gaining new skills to building confidence and empathy.
  • Fun and Fulfillment: Experience the joy and fulfillment that comes from knowing you're making a difference in the lives of others.

How You Can Help

  • Distribution: Assist in distributing menstrual kits to underserved communities, schools, and healthcare facilities.
  • Awareness: Spread awareness about menstrual health and hygiene through educational workshops and campaigns.
  • Fundraising: Organize fundraising events or campaigns to support our initiatives and reach more people in need.
  • Advocacy: Advocate for policy changes and initiatives that promote menstrual equity and access to menstrual products.
