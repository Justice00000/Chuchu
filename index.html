FAQs

Are ChuChu Softies sanitary pads suitable for all skin types?
Yes, our sanitary pads are designed to be gentle on all skin types, ensuring maximum comfort and safety during your menstrual cycle.
How do I wash and care for ChuChu Softies sanitary pads?
Simply rinse the pad with cold water after use, then hand wash or machine wash with mild detergent. Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach. Air dry or tumble dry on low heat.
Are ChuChu Softies sanitary pads reusable?
Yes, our pads are reusable and can be washed and reused for up to several years with proper care, making them an eco-friendly alternative to disposable pads.
Do ChuChu Softies sanitary pads provide adequate protection?
Yes, our pads feature a soft, breathable top layer and a comfortable, leak-proof backing, ensuring a comfortable and secure fit throughout the day.
Do ChuChu Softies sanitary pads come in different sizes?
Yes, we offer a range of sizes to suit your needs, from panty liners to overnight pads, providing the perfect fit for every stage of your menstrual cycle.
Are ChuChu Softies sanitary pads environmentally friendly?
Absolutely! By choosing reusable pads, you're helping to reduce waste and minimize your environmental impact, making them a sustainable choice for both you and the planet.
Can I use ChuChu Softies sanitary pads for sensitive skin?
Yes, our pads are hypoallergenic and free from harsh chemicals, making them ideal for sensitive skin.
