The Global Business School Network 2023

The Global Business School Network competition 2023 hosted over 307 participants in 87 teams from 58 schools in 17 African countries. The competition lasted 6 weeks and was concluded by a final pitch presentation which 11 international judges led. ChuChu Softies emerged in the first place and won a $5000 prize.
